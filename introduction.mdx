# Welcome to Memento Calendar

Memento Calendar is a minimalist mobile app that helps you visualize the passage of time through beautiful grid-based timelines. Inspired by Stoic philosophy, the app transforms abstract time into tangible visual grids where each square represents a day.

<Note>
  Available for iOS and Android
</Note>

## Core Philosophy

Memento Calendar embraces Stoic minimalism with a focus on awareness over motivation:

- **Clean, Distraction-Free Interface**: Dark-mode-first design with minimal UI elements
- **Tangible Time Visualization**: See your days laid out in a grid where each square tells a story
- **No Gamification**: No streaks, badges, or notifications—just pure awareness
- **Local-First**: Your data stays on your device with AsyncStorage persistence

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Timeline">
    Choose from preset options (current year, month, week) or create a custom date range.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set as Active">
    Select which timeline you want to display on your home screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Watch Time Pass">
    Each day that passes is marked in the grid, making time visible and tangible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Timeline Types Explained

**YEAR Timelines**  
Fixed year-long timelines (e.g., 2026) showing all 365 or 366 days of the year.

**MONTH Timelines**  
Month-specific timelines (e.g., March 2026) displaying every day of that month.

**WEEK Timelines**  
Auto-updating timelines that always show the current week (Monday through Sunday).

**CUSTOM Timelines**  
User-defined date ranges for tracking specific periods like projects, goals, or life events.

## Technology Stack

Memento Calendar is built with modern React Native technology:

- **React Native 0.81.5** with **Expo SDK 54**
- **Expo Router 6.0** for file-based navigation
- **AsyncStorage** for local data persistence
- **React Native Reanimated 4.1** for smooth animations
- **TypeScript** with strict mode enabled

<Note>
  The app is currently in MVP stage with local-only storage. Firebase sync and premium features are planned for future releases.
</Note>

## Design Principles

**Dark-Mode-First**  
The app defaults to dark mode, providing a calm and focused visual experience.

**Stoic Minimalism**  
Every design decision prioritizes clarity and purpose over decoration.

**Firebase-Ready Architecture**  
All data uses ISO 8601 date formats and flat structures, ready for future cloud sync.
