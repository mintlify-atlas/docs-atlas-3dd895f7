## What are Timelines?

Timelines are the core concept in Stoic Calendar. Each timeline represents a specific period of time, visualized as a grid where one dot equals one real day. Timelines make time passage visible and tangible.

<Info>
Only one timeline can be **active** at a time. The active timeline is displayed on your Home screen.
</Info>

## The 4 Timeline Types

Stoic Calendar supports four distinct timeline types, each designed for different time-tracking needs:

### Year Timeline

Tracks a complete calendar year from January 1st to December 31st.

- **Duration**: 365 or 366 days (leap years)
- **Example**: "2026"
- **Use case**: Visualize an entire year at once, see how much of the year has passed
- **Behavior**: Fixed dates, does not auto-update

<Tip>
Year timelines are perfect for annual goal tracking or seeing your year at a glance.
</Tip>

### Month Timeline

Tracks a single calendar month from the 1st to the last day of that month.

- **Duration**: 28-31 days depending on the month
- **Example**: "January 2026"
- **Use case**: Focus on the current month's progress
- **Behavior**: Fixed dates, does not auto-update

### Week Timeline

Tracks the current week from Monday to Sunday.

- **Duration**: 7 days
- **Example**: "This Week"
- **Use case**: Short-term daily awareness
- **Behavior**: Auto-updates to always show the current week

<Note>
Week timelines automatically update to the current week when you open the app. They always run Monday through Sunday.
</Note>

### Custom Timeline

Tracks any user-defined date range.

- **Duration**: Any period you choose
- **Example**: "Project X", "Summer Break", "Q1 2026"
- **Use case**: Track specific projects, events, or personal milestones
- **Behavior**: Fixed dates you define

<Tip>
Custom timelines are ideal for tracking project deadlines, trips, or any meaningful period in your life.
</Tip>

## Timeline States

Every timeline tracks three key metrics:

- **Days Passed**: How many days have already occurred
- **Days Remaining**: How many days are left in the timeline
- **Progress Percentage**: Visual representation of completion (0-100%)

<Info>
All dates in Stoic Calendar use ISO 8601 format for precise time tracking across time zones.
</Info>

## Switching Active Timelines

You can switch which timeline appears on your Home screen:

1. Go to the **Timelines** tab
2. Tap any timeline to make it active
3. The Home screen immediately updates to show your selected timeline

<Note>
When you activate a timeline, your previous active timeline is automatically deactivated. Only one timeline can be active at a time.
</Note>
