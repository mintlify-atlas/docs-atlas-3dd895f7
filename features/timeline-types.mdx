Stoic Calendar offers four timeline types to help you visualize time at different scales. Each timeline displays as a grid where every square represents one day, making time passage tangible and clear.

## Year Timeline

Track an entire year from January 1st to December 31st.

<Card title="Perfect for" icon="calendar-days">
  Annual goals, year-long projects, and seeing the full picture of your year
</Card>

### How it Works

- **Total Days**: 365 days (366 in leap years)
- **Title Format**: Displays the year (e.g., "2026")
- **Grid Layout**: Automatically calculated to fit your screen
- **Auto-Update**: Can be created for any past, present, or future year

### Creating a Year Timeline

1. Navigate to the Timelines screen
2. Tap the "+" button
3. Select "Year"
4. Choose the year you want to track
5. The timeline will be created with the title matching the year (e.g., "2026")

<Note>
  Year timelines display a fixed 365-day period. If you want to track "the current year" that updates automatically, create a new year timeline each January.
</Note>

---

## Month Timeline

Focus on a single month to track short-term goals and habits.

<Card title="Perfect for" icon="calendar-week">
  Monthly challenges, habit tracking, and sprint planning
</Card>

### How it Works

- **Total Days**: 28-31 days depending on the month
- **Title Format**: Displays the month name (e.g., "January", "February")
- **Grid Layout**: Typically 4-5 rows representing weeks
- **Auto-Update**: The current month timeline automatically updates when a new month begins

### Creating a Month Timeline

1. Navigate to the Timelines screen
2. Tap the "+" button
3. Select "Month"
4. Choose the month and year
5. The timeline will be created with the month name as the title

<Tip>
  Month timelines are great for building consistency. Seeing each day as a square makes it easier to maintain streaks and notice patterns.
</Tip>

---

## Week Timeline

Track the current week from Monday to Sunday.

<Card title="Perfect for" icon="calendar-range">
  Weekly sprints, immediate focus, and short-term accountability
</Card>

### How it Works

- **Total Days**: 7 days (Monday through Sunday)
- **Title**: Always displays as "This Week"
- **Grid Layout**: Single row of 7 squares
- **Auto-Update**: Automatically updates to the current week when a new week begins

### Creating a Week Timeline

1. Navigate to the Timelines screen
2. Tap the "+" button
3. Select "Week"
4. The timeline will be created for the current week

<Warning>
  Week timelines always track the **current week**. The app automatically detects when a new week starts and updates the timeline dates. This ensures your week timeline always shows Monday-Sunday of the current week.
</Warning>

### Week Boundaries

Week timelines use the Monday-Sunday convention:
- **Week Start**: Monday at 00:00:00
- **Week End**: Sunday at 23:59:59

## Custom Timeline

Create a timeline for any specific date range that matters to you.

<Card title="Perfect for" icon="calendar-plus">
  Project deadlines, countdowns to events, trip planning, or any custom time period
</Card>

### How it Works

- **Total Days**: Any number of days you specify
- **Title**: Your custom title (e.g., "Launch Project", "Summer Break", "Training Program")
- **Grid Layout**: Calculated based on total days
- **No Auto-Update**: Custom timelines remain fixed to your chosen dates

### Creating a Custom Timeline

1. Navigate to the Timelines screen
2. Tap the "+" button
3. Select "Custom"
4. Choose your start date and end date
5. Enter a custom title for your timeline
6. The timeline will be created with your exact specifications

<Tip>
  Custom timelines are incredibly flexible. Use them for:
  - Project deadlines ("Product Launch - 90 days")
  - Life events ("Days until graduation")
  - Seasonal tracking ("Summer 2026")
  - Training programs ("12-week fitness plan")
</Tip>

## Timeline Statistics

All timeline types display the same progress metrics:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Days Passed" icon="check">
    The number of complete days from the start date until today
  </Card>
  <Card title="Days Remaining" icon="hourglass">
    The number of days from today until the end date
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total Days" icon="calendar">
    The complete duration of the timeline from start to end
  </Card>
  <Card title="Progress Percentage" icon="chart-line">
    Completion percentage (0-100%) calculated from days passed
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Progress Calculation

The progress percentage is calculated as:

```typescript
progressPercentage = (daysPassed / totalDays) * 100
```

- **0%**: Timeline just started
- **50%**: Halfway through the timeline
- **100%**: Timeline completed

## Active Timeline

Only one timeline can be **active** at a time. The active timeline is displayed on your Home screen.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View on Home Screen">
    Your active timeline appears as a full grid on the Home screen
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch Active Timeline">
    Tap any timeline card in the Timelines screen to make it active
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic Switch">
    When you switch timelines, the previous active timeline is automatically deactivated
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The active timeline indicator appears as a blue accent on the timeline card in the Timelines list.
</Note>

## Timeline Management

### Creating Multiple Timelines

- **Free**: Create up to 3 timelines (including 1 custom timeline)
- **Pro**: Create unlimited timelines and unlimited custom timelines

### Editing Timelines

- Year and Month timelines can have their date range adjusted
- Week timelines cannot be edited (they auto-update)
- Custom timelines can have their title, start date, and end date changed

### Deleting Timelines

Swipe left on any timeline in the Timelines screen to reveal the delete option. Deleting a timeline is permanent and cannot be undone.

<Warning>
  If you delete your active timeline, you'll need to select a new active timeline from your remaining timelines.
</Warning>

## Technical Details

### Date Format

All timeline dates are stored in ISO 8601 format:
```
2026-01-01T00:00:00.000Z
```

### Timeline ID

Each timeline has a unique identifier generated at creation time.

### Timeline Validation

The app validates that:
- Start date is before end date
- All required fields are present
- Dates are valid calendar dates
