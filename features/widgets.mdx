Stoic Calendar widgets bring your active timeline directly to your iPhone's home screen and lock screen. See your progress at a glance without opening the app.

<Note>
  Widgets require a **development build** of Stoic Calendar. They do not work in Expo Go.
</Note>

---

## Home Screen Widgets

Home screen widgets display your active timeline's grid visualization, showing days passed and remaining.

### Available Sizes

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Small" icon="square">
    **All Users**
    
    Compact grid view with progress percentage
  </Card>
  <Card title="Medium" icon="rectangle-wide">
    **All Users**
    
    Larger grid with timeline title and stats
  </Card>
  <Card title="Large" icon="square-full">
    **Pro Only**
    
    Full grid visualization with detailed progress
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Large widgets require a Pro subscription. Free users can use small and medium widgets.
</Warning>

### Adding a Home Screen Widget

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Edit Mode">
    Long-press on your home screen until apps start jiggling
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Widget">
    Tap the "+" button in the top-left corner
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Stoic Calendar">
    Search for "Stoic Calendar" or scroll to find it in the widget list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Size">
    Select Small, Medium, or Large (Pro only)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Home Screen">
    Drag the widget to your desired location and tap "Done"
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Steps>

  </Step>

    Choose the lock screen you want to edit
  </Step>

## Widget Data Sync

Widgets display data from your active timeline and sync automatically with the main app.

### What Widgets Display

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Timeline Information">
    - Timeline title (e.g., "2026", "This Week", "Summer Goals")
    - Timeline type (Year, Month, Week, or Custom)
    - Start and end dates
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Progress Statistics">
    - Days passed
    - Days remaining
    - Total days
    - Progress percentage (0-100%)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Visual Grid">
    - Grid squares representing each day
    - Filled squares for passed days
    - Empty squares for remaining days
    - Color theme matching your app settings
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Sync Behavior

Widgets automatically sync when:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="App Launch" icon="power-off">
    Every time you open Stoic Calendar
  </Card>
  <Card title="Timeline Switch" icon="arrows-rotate">
    When you change your active timeline
  </Card>
  <Card title="Timeline Update" icon="pen-to-square">
    When week/month timelines auto-update
  </Card>
  <Card title="Settings Change" icon="gear">
    When you change theme or color settings
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  Widgets use iOS App Groups to share data between the main app and widget extensions. This ensures your widget always displays up-to-date information.
</Info>

## Widget Configuration

### Timeline Selection

By default, widgets display your **active timeline**. The active timeline is the one shown on your Home screen in the main app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Stoic Calendar">
    Launch the Stoic Calendar app
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Timelines">
    Tap the Timelines tab at the bottom
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Timeline">
    Tap any timeline to make it active
  </Step>
  <Step title="Widget Updates">
    Your widgets will automatically update to show the new active timeline
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can create multiple timelines and switch between them anytime. Your widgets will always reflect your currently active timeline.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Widget shows 'No Timeline'">
    **Solution**: Open the app and create at least one timeline, then make it active by tapping on it in the Timelines screen.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Widget not updating">
    **Solutions**:
    1. Open the Stoic Calendar app to trigger a sync
    2. Remove and re-add the widget
    3. Restart your iPhone
    4. Ensure you're using a development build (not Expo Go)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Widget shows wrong timeline">
    **Solution**: Open the app, go to Timelines, and tap the timeline you want to display. Only the active timeline appears in widgets.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Widget colors don't match app">
    **Solution**: Sync may be delayed. Open the app to force a sync. If the issue persists, try changing your color theme in Settings and changing it back.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't add large widget">
    **Solution**: Large widgets require a Pro subscription. Upgrade in Settings → Upgrade to Pro.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Implementation

### App Groups

Widgets use iOS App Groups for data sharing:

```
group.com.stoiccalendar.shared
```

The main app writes widget data to the shared container, and widget extensions read from it.

### Data Format

Widget data is stored as JSON in the App Groups container:

```typescript
interface WidgetTimelineData {
  id: string;
  type: string; // 'year' | 'month' | 'week' | 'custom'
  title: string;
  startDate: string; // ISO 8601
  endDate: string; // ISO 8601
  daysPassed: number;
  daysRemaining: number;
  totalDays: number;
  progressPercentage: number; // 0-100
}
```

### Widget Reload

The app calls `ExtensionStorage.reloadWidget()` to trigger immediate widget updates after:

- Timeline changes
- Theme changes
- Pro status changes

For implementation details, see `services/widget-data-service.ts:108`.
