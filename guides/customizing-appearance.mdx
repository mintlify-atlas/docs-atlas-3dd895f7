# Customizing Appearance

Stoic Calendar offers appearance customization options that let you personalize the visual experience while maintaining the minimalist design philosophy.

## Changing the Theme

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap the **Settings** icon (gear icon) in the top-right corner of the home screen, or navigate to the Settings tab in the bottom tab bar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the Appearance section">
    Scroll to the **Appearance** section. You'll see three theme options displayed as horizontal buttons with icons.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your preferred theme">
    Choose from three theme modes:

    - **System**: Automatically matches your device's system theme (light or dark). The theme changes when you change your device settings.
    - **Light**: Always uses light mode with a white background and dark text, regardless of your system settings.
    - **Dark**: Always uses dark mode with a black background and light text, regardless of your system settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View the changes">
    The theme applies immediately when you tap an option. You'll see:

    - A checkmark on the selected theme button
    - A subtle glow effect around the active theme
    - The entire app interface updates to reflect your choice
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Theme Modes

### System Theme

The **System** theme is ideal if you:

- Use automatic dark mode on your device (e.g., dark mode after sunset)
- Want the app to match other apps on your device
- Prefer not to manage theme settings in individual apps

The app will automatically detect your device's current theme and switch between light and dark mode accordingly.

### Light Theme

The **Light** theme provides:

- Clean white backgrounds
- Dark gray text for excellent readability
- Subtle shadows and separators
- Bright accent colors

Best for use in bright environments or if you prefer light interfaces.

### Dark Theme

The **Dark** theme features:

- True black backgrounds
- Light gray text
- Reduced eye strain in low-light conditions
- Dimmed accent colors that don't overwhelm

Stoic Calendar defaults to dark mode, reflecting the app's calm, contemplative design philosophy.

## Customizing Grid Colors

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Grid Colors">
    In the Settings screen, scroll to the **Grid Colors** section below Appearance. You'll see four color palette options displayed as small grid previews.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview the color palettes">
    Each palette shows a mini 4x4 grid with dots in the filled and empty colors. This preview helps you visualize how the palette will look on your timeline grid.

    Available palettes:

    - **Classic**: The default palette with neutral colors that work well in both light and dark mode
    - **Forest**: Earthy greens inspired by nature
    - **Sunset**: Warm oranges and purples reminiscent of sunset
    - **Monochrome**: High-contrast black and white for minimalist aesthetics
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a color palette">
    Tap any palette to apply it. The selected palette shows:

    - A border highlight with the accent color
    - A subtle glow effect
    - The palette name below the preview
  </Step>

  <Step title="Return to the home screen">
    Go back to the home screen to see your timeline grid with the new colors. The grid updates immediately:

    - **Filled dots** represent days that have passed, shown in the palette's filled color
    - **Empty dots** represent upcoming days, shown in the palette's empty color
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Color Palette Details

### Classic

The default balanced palette:

- **Dark mode**: Warm gray filled dots, dark gray empty dots
- **Light mode**: Dark gray filled dots, light gray empty dots
- Works well for all timeline types
- Neutral and non-distracting

### Forest

Nature-inspired greens:

- **Dark mode**: Sage green filled dots, muted dark green empty dots
- **Light mode**: Deep green filled dots, light mint empty dots
- Creates a calm, grounded feeling
- Ideal for users who appreciate natural aesthetics

### Sunset

Warm and vibrant:

- **Dark mode**: Burnt orange filled dots, deep purple empty dots
- **Light mode**: Bright orange filled dots, soft purple empty dots
- Adds energy and warmth to the interface
- Great for users who want more color personality

### Monochrome

High-contrast minimalism:

- **Dark mode**: White filled dots, dark gray empty dots
- **Light mode**: Black filled dots, light gray empty dots
- Maximum clarity and focus
- Perfect for users who prefer extreme simplicity

## How Color Palettes Adapt to Themes

Each color palette includes two sets of colors:

- One optimized for dark mode
- One optimized for light mode

When you switch themes, the grid colors automatically adjust to maintain proper contrast and visual harmony. You don't need to manually change color palettes when switching between light and dark mode.

## Design Philosophy

All customization options in Stoic Calendar are designed to:

- Maintain visual clarity and focus
- Reduce distractions
- Support contemplation of time passage
- Never include gamification elements or notifications

The color palettes are carefully chosen to be:

- Easy on the eyes for extended viewing
- Neutral enough not to demand attention
- Varied enough to offer personal expression

As the app philosophy states: "Time is presented neutrally, calmly, and honestly."
