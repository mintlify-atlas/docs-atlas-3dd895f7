# Managing Timelines

Stoic Calendar lets you manage multiple timelines and switch between them easily. You can edit custom timelines, delete timelines you no longer need, and set any timeline as your active timeline.

## Switching Between Timelines

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the timeline dropdown">
    On the home screen, tap the timeline title at the top. A dropdown menu appears showing all your timelines with a preview of each one.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a timeline">
    Tap any timeline in the list to make it your active timeline. The dropdown closes automatically, and the home screen updates to display the selected timeline with its grid visualization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The active timeline is the one displayed on your home screen. Only one timeline can be active at a time. The active timeline appears first in all timeline lists.

## Using the Timeline Management Screen

For more comprehensive timeline management, use the Timeline Management screen:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open timeline management">
    There are two ways to access timeline management:

    - From the home screen: Tap the timeline title, then tap **Manage Timelines...** at the bottom of the dropdown
    - From settings: Navigate to the Settings tab, then tap **Manage Timelines** under the Timelines section
  </Step>

  <Step title="View all timelines">
    The Timeline Management screen displays all your timelines as cards with:

    - Timeline title and date range
    - Visual grid preview showing progress
    - Progress text (X of Y days)
    - Days remaining
    - Percentage passed

    Cards appear with staggered fade-in animations for a smooth experience.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a timeline to make it active">
    Tap any timeline card to set it as your active timeline. You'll feel a haptic feedback, and the screen will close automatically, returning you to the home screen with your newly selected timeline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Timelines

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open timeline options">
    In the Timeline Management screen, find the timeline you want to edit. Tap the edit button (three dots or edit icon) on the timeline card.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Modify timeline details">
    The timeline edit form appears with the current values pre-filled:

    - **Title**: Update the timeline name (for custom timelines)
    - **Start Date**: Change the start date (for custom timelines)
    - **End Date**: Change the end date (for custom timelines)

    Note: You cannot edit the type of a timeline after creation. Year, Month, and Week timelines have fixed structures and can only be edited through their title field when editing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Tap **Save** to update the timeline. The changes take effect immediately, and if this was your active timeline, the home screen updates accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting Timelines

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a timeline to delete">
    You can delete timelines from two places:

    - **From the dropdown**: Tap the timeline title on the home screen, then tap the delete icon next to any timeline
    - **From Timeline Management**: Open the Timeline Management screen and tap the delete button on any timeline card
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    A confirmation dialog appears asking if you're sure you want to delete the timeline. The dialog shows the timeline title and warns that this action cannot be undone.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete deletion">
    Tap **Delete** to permanently remove the timeline. If you change your mind, tap **Cancel**.

    If you delete your active timeline, the app automatically selects another timeline as active. If you delete your last timeline, the app creates a default Year timeline for the current year.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You cannot delete a timeline if it's your only timeline. The app always maintains at least one timeline.
</Note>

## Creating Additional Timelines

From the Timeline Management screen, you can create new timelines:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Create Timeline button">
    At the bottom of the timeline list, tap the **Create Timeline** button with the "+" icon.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Follow the creation process">
    The timeline creation form appears. Follow the steps in the [Creating Timelines](/guides/creating-timelines) guide to set up your new timeline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Timeline Limits

Free users can manage up to 3 timelines. If you reach this limit:

- Tapping **Create Timeline** opens the upgrade screen
- You must delete a timeline before creating a new one, or upgrade to Pro

Pro users have unlimited timelines and can create as many as they need.

## Auto-Updating Timelines

Week, Month, and Year timelines automatically update when you open the app:

- **Week timelines** update to the current week (Monday-Sunday) if the week has changed
- **Month timelines** update to the current month if the month has changed
- **Year timelines** update to the current year if the year has changed

This ensures your automatic timelines always reflect the current time period. Custom timelines never auto-update and always maintain the dates you set.
