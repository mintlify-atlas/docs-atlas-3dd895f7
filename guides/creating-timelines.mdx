# Creating Timelines

Stoic Calendar allows you to create multiple timelines to track different time periods. You can create automatic timelines for years, months, and weeks, or create custom timelines with your own date ranges.

## Creating Your First Timeline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the timeline dropdown">
    On the home screen, tap the timeline title at the top (it displays the name of your current timeline, like "2026"). A dropdown menu will appear showing all your timelines.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap the + button">
    In the dropdown menu, tap the "+" button to create a new timeline. The timeline creation form will slide up from the bottom.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your timeline type">
    Select one of four timeline types:

    - **Year**: Tracks the current year (365 or 366 days)
    - **Month**: Tracks the current month (28-31 days)
    - **Week**: Tracks the current week (7 days, Monday-Sunday)
    - **Custom**: Create your own timeline with any date range
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure custom settings (if applicable)">
    If you selected **Custom** timeline:

    - **Title**: Enter a name for your timeline (up to 80 characters)
    - **Start Date**: Tap to select the first day of your timeline
    - **End Date**: Tap to select the last day of your timeline

    For Year, Month, and Week timelines, the title and dates are automatically set based on the current date.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your timeline">
    Tap the **Save** button in the top-right corner. Your new timeline will be created and automatically set as the active timeline on your home screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Timeline Types Explained

### Year Timeline

A Year timeline automatically tracks the entire current year from January 1 to December 31. The title displays the year (e.g., "2026").

The app prevents you from creating duplicate Year timelines for the same year.

### Month Timeline

A Month timeline tracks the current month from the 1st to the last day (28-31 days depending on the month). The title displays the month name and year (e.g., "January 2026").

The app prevents you from creating duplicate Month timelines for the same month and year.

### Week Timeline

A Week timeline tracks the current week from Monday to Sunday (7 days). The title displays the date range (e.g., "Jan 20 - Jan 26, 2026").

Week timelines automatically update to the current week when you open the app. The app prevents you from creating duplicate Week timelines.

### Custom Timeline

A Custom timeline allows you to track any date range you choose. This is perfect for:

- Project deadlines
- Personal goals or challenges
- Academic semesters
- Life milestones
- Any other time period you want to visualize

You can create multiple custom timelines with overlapping dates.

## Understanding the Creation Form

The timeline creation form uses progressive disclosure:

- When you first open the form, you'll see the four timeline type buttons
- If you select **Custom**, additional fields appear for title, start date, and end date
- Date pickers use the native iOS/Android date picker for familiar interaction
- The **Cancel** button closes the form without saving
- The **Save** button creates the timeline and makes it active

## Premium Limits

Free users can create up to 3 timelines. If you reach this limit and try to create another timeline, you'll be prompted to upgrade to Pro for unlimited timelines.

Pro users have no limits on the number of timelines they can create.
